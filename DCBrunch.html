Brunch Spots in DC!

801

801 is a delicious, black-owned brunch spot located in the Shaw neighborhood. Not only does their bottomless brunch come with a flight of juices, but they also serve the best lobster mac and cheese bites!

Pinstripes

Located in the heart of Georgetown, Pinstripes bottomless mimosas never disappoint! Their mini waffles with fresh berries are also a must have!

Peacock Cafe

Another favorite in Georgetown is Peacock Cafe, a spot frequented by diplomats and politicians. The shoe string fries are scrumptious!

Martin's Tavern

The famous spot where JFK proposed to Jackie O! The brunch is also a show stopper, especially the potato and egg skillet!

Tatte Bakery

Tatte, located in the West End, is a chic brunch stop with sweet, delectable pastries and savory sandwiches! It is also the perfect spot for social media worthy photos!
